Notes

A collection of typeset notes, exercises, and course materials.

  • Measure Theory & Integration PDF
    Note transcribed from UBC's MATH 507 (Winter Term 1, 2025/26). Covers σ-algebras, measures and outer measures, Borel measures, the Lebesgue integral, convergence theorems, product measures and the Fubini-Tonelli theorems, signed measures, the Radon-Nikodym theorem, and differentiation.
  • Functional Analysis PDF
    Notes transcribed from UBC's MATH 510 (Winter Term 2, 2025/26). Covers Banach spaces, bounded linear maps and functionals, the Baire category theorem and its consequences, Lp and Hilbert spaces, weak and weak* topologies, the Fourier transform and Schwartz class, Fourier series, and further topics including interpolation theory and distribution theory.
  • Probability Theory PDF
    Notes drawn from UBC's STAT 547C (Winter Term 1, 2025/26). Covers the measure-theoretic foundations of probability, probability kernels, conditioning and independence, concentration inequalities, integral probability metrics, φ-divergences.
  • Folland Exercises PDF
    Selected solutions to exercises from Folland's Real Analysis: Modern Techniques and Their Applications.
